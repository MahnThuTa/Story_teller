BAGAN

See More

When I said Myanmar, the first thing I saw was Bagan, one of the world heritage sites. It is a region where Buddhism, the country's main religion, flourished. That's why I went to Bagan first. When I arrived Bagan, the smell of delicious food attracted me. Even though I ate on the plane, I felt hungry. But I just went to the hotel because I was very tired. The next day, I decided to look around Bagan. I went there first because Ananda Buddha is famous. When I arrived there, I felt peace, the sound of the wind, Everything was silent except the sound of birds. After visiting the temple for over an hour, I got hungry, so I decided to go back to the hotel. On the way back to the hotel, when I first arrived in Bagan, I got another tasteful smell. Because of that smell, I can't control my appetite anymore. Since I am a Food rampant from the beginning, I started eating a lot of Hta-min-thote.

After eating the food from Bagan, I was very full. So I decided to go for a walk. The pagodas of Bagan always give me peace. After visiting one pagoda after another, I wanted to know more about Bagan's history, so I hired a guide who knows Bagan's history well. He told me that Bagan is a World Heritage Site (UNESCO). He started talking about King Anoratha, the founder of Bagan. I was interested in the history of Bagan and didn't even notice that it was getting late. That's why I went back to the hotel to listen to the rest the next day. After visiting a lot, I can't do anything when I got to the hotel. After dinner, I went to sleep.

When I arrived the next morning, my stomach hurt a lot, so I went to the doctor and he said this happened cause I ate a lot of spicy food. And he is not wrong with it. The next day, my stomach was still not feeling well, but I couldn't take another day off, so today I went to a nearby village. The village is not far from the hotel, and we arrived in a short time. As soon as he reached the village, the children of the village came running. I was startled as to why, and then someone who spoke English asked me, They said that when foreigners come, they rush like that. It was almost evening when I arrived, so I decided to stay at the old man's house for the night. The next day is the day I will say goodbye to this place. I am sure that I will come to this place again.
